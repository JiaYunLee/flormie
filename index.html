Collect a flower

core user flower include snap a picture, identify, and shrinking into floating stickers (or flormie) on the canvas.

Interactive canvas

Each flower is transformed into a collectible sticker that responds to the user's motion. Instead of simply storing stickers, the canvas creates a playful and personal space where users can interact with their collection. Seeing flowers accumulate over time also reinforces a sense of progress and encourages continued exploration.

Flower card

Every discovery is saved as a flower card containing key information, including the date, location, and flower meaning. Users can easily review and revisit their collection over time.

Different views

I designed additional views for the collection. On the left, a grid view displays stickers and names for easy browsing. On the right, a map view shows where each flower was discovered, since the app stores location, helping users revisit their memories.
