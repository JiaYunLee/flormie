privacy policy

flormie helps users identify flowers, save discoveries, and organize entries they create in the app. This page explains what information may be collected and how it is used.

Information We May Collect

Depending on which features you use, the app may collect:

  • Photos you choose to capture or upload for flower identification.
  • Location data, if you grant permission, to save where a flower was found.
  • Basic account or sign-in information if authentication is enabled.
  • Purchase information related to in-app purchases, as provided by Apple.
  • Diagnostic or usage information needed to operate, secure, or improve the app.

How Information Is Used

  • To identify flowers and generate results you request.
  • To save and display your flower entries and related metadata.
  • To provide app functionality such as location-based memories and purchase restoration.
  • To maintain the app, troubleshoot issues, and improve performance.

Photos and Location

Photos and location data are only used to support the features you choose to use. You can deny location access in your device settings at any time.

Third-Party Services

The app may rely on third-party services to process identification requests, support app functionality, or handle purchases. Those services may process data only as needed to provide their service.

Data Retention

Data may be retained for as long as needed to provide the app and comply with legal obligations. If you want data deleted, contact us at the email below.

Your Choices

  • You can stop using the app at any time.
  • You can revoke camera or location permissions in iOS Settings.
  • You can contact us to request deletion or ask privacy questions.

Children's Privacy

The app is not intended for children under 13 without parental permission.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version will always be posted on this page.

Contact

For privacy questions or deletion requests, contact support@flormie.com.
